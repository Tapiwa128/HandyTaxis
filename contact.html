Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the best time to visit Victoria Falls?

The best time to visit Victoria Falls is from February to May, right after the summer rains, when the water flow is at its peak and the Falls are most spectacular. However, each season offers a different experience, so it depends on your preferences.

Do I need a visa to visit Zimbabwe?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Many visitors can obtain a visa on arrival, but we recommend checking with the Zimbabwean embassy or consulate in your country before traveling. We can also assist with visa information as part of our booking service.

What should I pack for my safari?

We recommend neutral-colored clothing, a hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, binoculars, a camera, comfortable walking shoes, and a light jacket for early morning game drives. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.

Are your safaris suitable for children?

Yes, we offer family-friendly safaris with activities suitable for children. Some activities have age restrictions, but we can tailor your itinerary to include age-appropriate experiences that the whole family will enjoy.
